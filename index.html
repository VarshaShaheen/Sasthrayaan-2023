About

"Reaching out to the masses, Reaching out to Excellence"

Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT), re-accredited by NAAC with A+ grade is organizing this third edition of SASTHRAYAAN to showcase the vibrant and active research activities carried out in its various departments. The state-of-the-art facilities available in CUSAT will be exhibited with live demonstrations, working models and presentations. Students who are especially interested in Science and Engineering fields will get a valuable exposure on various cutting-edge research areas. Multiple exhibition stalls will be arranged at CUSAT Seminar complex along with illuminating visits to numerous research laboratories and departments in CUSAT campus. There will be expert talks in various topics including career orientation, various programmes in CUSAT, Industry Academia partnership etc. We welcome all of you to grace the occasion.
