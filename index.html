About

In order to facilitate the General Public with the vast knowledge built up by Cochin University of Science and Technology (CUSAT) through its Research and Advanced Learning Activities, a Three Day Scientific and Technological Exhibition - "Sasthrayaan 2023", is organized in collaboration with Rashtriya Uchchatar Shiksha Abhiyan (RUSA), is scheduled to be conducted on 23rd to 25th of January 2023. The Seminar Complex at University Main Campus, Kalamassery, Cochin, will be the venue for the event. Exhibition, Science Conference, Discussions, Project Demonstrations and associated display, etc together compose Sasthrayaan 2023. Industrialists, Businessmen, Politicians, Commoners, NGOs, Resident Associations, Students from Schools and Colleges, and all interested people can attend the event at Zero Registration Fees.
