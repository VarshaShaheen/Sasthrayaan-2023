Instruction to the participants

  • CUSAT will be open to school/college students and the public during the Sastrayaan event, taking place from January 23-25, 2023. All university departments, laboratories, and research facilities will be accessible during this time.
  • All participants are required to report to the Registration Desk at the CUSAT Seminar Complex upon arrival.
  • Participants can obtain a token for visiting the departments at the Registration Desk.
  • CUSAT volunteers will guide participants to the various places of visit.
  • Food will be available for purchase on a payment basis at the CUSAT Open Air Auditorium, in association with CUSAT restaurant.
      • Vegetarian meals are priced at Rs. 30/-
      Non-vegetarian meals are priced at Rs. 60/-"
      Food coupons can be purchased from registration counter
  • In case of any assistance, please contact the volunteers or coordinators present on the campus.
